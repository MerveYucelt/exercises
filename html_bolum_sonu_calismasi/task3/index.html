...Chocolate Cubes...

Delicious easy-made dessert with chocolate and pistaccios... These little cubes are prepared with only 4 ingredients and served with colorful sheets and little sticks. These recipe is the best chose for crowded organizations like birthdays.

Recipe by: Rukiye Beyza SarÄ±kaya

For: 4 people

Preparing time: 30 minutes

Cooking time: 10 minutes


Ingredients..

  • 350 gr dark chocolate
  • 150 ml creme
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 150 gr pistaccios

How to make..

  1. Melt dark chocolate with steam table.
  2. Add creme and the butter to the melt chocolate
  3. Chop the pistaccios and mix with the chocolate
  4. Boil te mixture on to a square tray or a baking paper
  5. Refrigerate 2 hours
  6. Slice it little squares and serve it

Enjoy your meal
